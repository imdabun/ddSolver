Line no.    Description
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1           Hand on lead currently. North = 0, East = 1, etc.
2           Trump suit. NT = -1, Clubs = 0, Diamonds = 1, Hearts = 2, Spades = 3
3           Blank line
4 ~ 7       North hand, starting from spades, then hearts, diamonds, clubs
8           Blank line
9 ~ 12      East hand in similar order.
13          Blank line
14 ~ 17     South hand in similar order.
18          Blank line
19 ~ 22     West hand in similar order.

Cards must be white space separated. For examples please refer to any hand in 
this folder.
